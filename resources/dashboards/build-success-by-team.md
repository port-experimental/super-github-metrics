# Build success by team
Rank teams by their build success rate across repositories they are owner of

## Prerequisite
- Teams should be ingested into Port
- Ownership between team, repositories, workflows and workflow runs should be set

## Steps
- Create a widget and select "Table"
- Blueprint: Workflow
- After creation, click on "Customize table" at the 3 dots located at the top right corner
- Click on the "group" icon which are three squares layered on each other. Group by owning teams
- Click on "sort" icon and sort by "Success Rate Last 30 days"
